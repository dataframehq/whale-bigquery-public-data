* [STRING]    `stop_name`
  - Full text name of the  transit stop
* [FLOAT]     `stop_lat`
  - Latitude of the stop
* [FLOAT]     `stop_lon`
  - Longitude of the stop
* [GEOGRAPHY] `stop_geom`
